About OCT Retinal Analysis


Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is a cutting-edge imaging technique used to capture detailed cross-sectional views of the retina. Our AI-powered platform applies deep learning to these scans for fast, accurate detection of retinal conditions such as CNV, DME, Drusen, and Normal cases.

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)is a non-invasive, high-resolution imaging technique that captures cross-sectional views of the retina. It plays a critical role in detecting and monitoring various retinal and macular diseases, offering detailed visualization of retinal layers down to microns in resolution.

Built on the highly efficient MobileNetV3 architecture, the model is trained on thousands of annotated OCT images, allowing it to deliver real-time, clinical-grade predictions. It also offers explainable insights via heatmaps and confidence scores, ensuring transparency and trust in AI decisions.The OCT Retinal Analysis platform bridges the gap between medical expertise and cutting-edge artificial intelligence, transforming how retinal diseases are detected and managed.

This platform empowers ophthalmologists, medical students, and researchers to accelerate diagnosis, support early intervention, and enhance retinal care outcomes through intelligent automation.
